THE WEIGHTING SYSTEM

The Weighting Sytem is a standard criteria used to determine whether a public university applicant is eligible to take a certain course based on their resulting cutoff points. Its mostly used by public universities in uganda that offer government scholarships to students

WEIGHTING CRITERIA

The maximum points a candidate can get is 53 except for girls whose weights are added an extra 1.5 to get 54.5

There are two result sets considered:

  • Lower Level (O'level) Results
  • High Level-High School (A'level) Results

For O'level (Contributes 3 points)

Ten subjects are done and each subject scores 0.3 points for Distinctions (D1,D2)
0.2 points are awarded for Credits (C3-C6)
0.1 ponits are given for passes (P7-P8) and 0 points for failure i.e (F9)

The best candidate scores 3 total points and worst 0 points

For A'level (Contributes 50 points)

A'level is the determining factor for the course to be taken at campus since it contributes 97% of the total weights

  • 5 subjects are done of which 3 main ones (determinig factor) carry a total of 48 points and the other two each carries 1 point
  • Of the three subjects, 2 are called essentials and determine the kind of course to be taken (e.g Mathematics & Physic for Engineering courses, Biology & Chemistry for medical related courses). These subjects each has a weight of 3
  • The other 1 subject is called relevant and carries 2 points
  • The remaining two subjects one called desirable and accompanies the main three subjects. These maybe (ICT or sub-math). The last subject is compulsory for every candidate (General Paper)
  • For A'level grades the conversion scale is A=6, B=5, C=4, D=3, E=2, O=1, F=0
  • Earlier we talked about the 48 points and this is for the three main subjects scored by the best candidate
  • To get that;
    • Essentials(2 subjects) => (2*3*6) = 36 i.e 2 -> number of subjects, 3 -> weights for an essential and 6 -> conversion scale for an A
    • Relevant(1 subjects) => (1*2*6) = 12
    • So; 36+12 = 48
    • 48 + 2 (One for desirable (ICT or Submath) and the other for General paper) = 50 points
